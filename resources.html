Mindfulness

What it is:
Mindfulness is the practice of being fully present and aware of your thoughts, emotions, and surroundings without judgment.

Benefits:
Reduced stress, improved emotional regulation, and enhanced self-awareness.

Key Links:
  • Kristin Neff – Self-Compassion
  • Padraig O Morain
  • Free Meditation App – Insight Timer

Practical Tools
  • Breathing Techniques: Box breathing, 4-7-8 breath
  • Tapping (EFT): Emotional Freedom Technique
  • Polyvagal Exercises: Vagus nerve activation, humming, cold exposure
  • Sleep / Deep relaxation: Try a Yoga Nidra session
