Therapy Services

Our Therapeutic Approach

At Therapy Journey Ireland, our therapists work at a deeper relational level—this takes time, trust, and patience.

We take a holistic approach, exploring the cognitive, emotional, and physical world of the person. Our services draw on a variety of therapeutic approaches, always tailored to the unique needs of the individual.

Relational & Person-Centred

We work in a person-centred way, meeting and accepting individuals wherever they are in their lives. Building a strong therapeutic relationship is central to the healing process, and this relationship often becomes the foundation for deeper self-exploration and transformation.

Gestalt & Creative Techniques

Gestalt therapy is often incorporated into our work. The focus is on what is presenting in the moment, helping to bring clarity and meaning to the experience. We also use a variety of creative techniques—such as visualisation, role-play, or body awareness—to support expression and understanding.

Neuroscience & Developmental Frameworks

We incorporate learnings from neuroscience, developmental psychology, and attachment theory to guide our work. These frameworks help us explore a person’s inner experience, support nervous system regulation, and understand patterns formed in early relationships.

Short-Term Therapy & CBT

For specific challenges or situations, short-term counselling may be appropriate. Typically spanning six to eight sessions, it may include elements of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) to explore thought and behaviour patterns.

Addiction Counselling & Group Support

Several of our therapists provide addiction counselling and also support families impacted by addiction. Many also facilitate therapeutic groups and special events throughout the year.

To learn more or inquire about upcoming groups, please contact the therapist directly.
